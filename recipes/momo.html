Mo:Mo

Discription

Momo is a type of steamed filled dumpling in Tibetan and Nepali cuisine that is also popular in neighbouring Bhutan and India

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Knead the dough.
  2. Fill the meat inside the dough.
  3. Put inside the utensil.
  4. Serve it hot and enjoy!
